Health Insurance Overview

Understanding your health insurance options and knowing how to navigate your policy can make a big difference in the quality of care you receive and how much you pay. Explore tips on using your insurance and essential information on policy terms, coverage, and more.

Using Your Insurance +

Understanding how to use your health insurance can help you save money and ensure you receive the best care possible. Here are a few tips:

  • Know Your Coverage: Understand what is covered and what is not. Check your plan details and policy documents.
  • Choose In-Network Providers: Visit doctors and hospitals within your insurer’s network to avoid higher costs.
  • Use Preventive Services: Many health plans cover preventive services like vaccinations and screenings at no additional cost.
  • Get Pre-Approval When Necessary: Some treatments require prior approval from your insurance provider. Be sure to check with them in advance.
Health Insurance Information +

Health insurance helps cover the cost of medical services. Here’s what you should know:

  • Premiums: This is the amount you pay monthly or annually for your insurance policy.
  • Deductibles: The amount you must pay out of pocket before your insurance begins to cover certain services.
  • Copayments & Coinsurance: Your share of costs after your deductible is met, often represented as a percentage.
  • Out-of-Pocket Maximum: The maximum amount you pay per year for covered services. After reaching this limit, insurance covers 100% of costs for covered services.
